Why I Write

I write because thinking in public is different from thinking in private. When you commit an idea to words someone else might read, you find out quickly how well you actually understand it.

My posts range from technical walkthroughs to personal essays to opinions I might regret later. I write about AI because I live in it. I write about other things because I'm a person first and a researcher second. The blog will grow as I have more to say.

If something I wrote was useful, wrong, or interesting to you - I'd genuinely like to know. Send me a note.
