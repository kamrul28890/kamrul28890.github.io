Multi-Agent Debate Intelligence Platform

An AI debate simulation platform engineered with compact LLM infrastructure and voice adaptation. The system uses Qwen 2.5 0.5B for controlled debate orchestration, supporting both policy-style and academic-style argumentation with character-specific voice delivery.

  • Integrated Qwen 2.5 0.5B for multi-agent response generation
  • Voice cloning workflows for realistic, character-specific delivery
  • Structured agent roles for policy and academic debate formats
  • Rhetorical quality evaluation under constrained compute
